Research Interests

My research interest is Cybersecurity, and related research areas fall on the intersection of Mobile Systems, Trustworthy AI, and Wireless Communications. As a cybersecurity researcher, I discover the vulnerabilities of existing mobile systems, design the countermeasures of advanced attacks targeted AI models and systems, and explore the potential innovations to boost the usability of real-world applications. My highlight research primarily focuses on Security, Privacy, and Usability.
